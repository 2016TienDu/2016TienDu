What We Need

ATRC always welcomes anyone who is good at / interested in these following tasks:

Computer Programming

As almost any studio who offer digital products, ATRC relies on numerous codes to establish the games, applications and websites. The more powerful our programming team, the stronger our ability.

Video Editing

From single clips to the whole promotion video, a competent video editor contributes a lot to videos we will show the world, as long as they know how to use editing applications such as Adobe Premiere Pro and After Effects.

Art Design

Despite having hired illustrators for our crucial artworks and illustrations, there is emerging requirement for us to seek exclusive artists. We do hope that costs for artworks can be reduced.

Dubbing Actors

As for some products such as a visual novel, it is a pity that no enough dubbers here for the main characters. We embrace proper dubbers with different roles including voiceovers and actors.
