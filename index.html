Who Are We?

Anime Technology Researching Centre (ATRC) is a non-profit interest studio established by four high school students. We, the members of ATRC, aim to show people around us the charm of Animation, Comic, and Games (ACG) while create our own work as the amateurs.

What We Do:

Members in ATRC also get opportunities to develop a lot of skills such as programming, picture & video editing, artwork designing, etc. As for the work we make, please refer to the section "Products". We are excited about almost anything related to the computer.

Our Members

“Click me for more introduction.”

Tien Du

The Programmer & Chief Designer

Du is one of the founders of ATRC, co-operating with Harry Wang to create
The Taboo Book, ATRC's first visual novel. Du's duty contains the programming and
main design of GUI, visual effects, and advertisement.

"I have always dreamed of engaging in game development since I was a child, and the
programming project in Grade 12 gave me a chance. In the process of programming,
Harry gave me a lot of help. We continued our cooperation since 2021 and created
ATRC studio together to find more partners for our game development."

Harry Wang

The Programmer & Public Relations Director

Wang is one of the founders of ATRC, co-operating with Tien Du to create
The Taboo Book, ATRC's first visual novel. Wang plays a significant role in leading the
programming process. He also contributes a lot to represent ATRC
to outsource illustrations and artworks in need.

"I am extremely excited about having our ATRC studio, and our The Taboo Book.
Luckily, The project offered us the chance of making our own game. So the four of us
started to work together... As a saying goes, Tech-Otakus save the world."

Emilia Fan

The Dubber & Proofreader

Fan dubs two important characters in the visual novel The Taboo Book. She is
ATRC's main dubber and patiently collate many text work such as the script.

"It's always been my dream to dub game characters. This is a good chance for me
to realize it and train myself. It's been a privilege to witness the birth of
a fictional character, and I'm happy to give them voice and let them exist in the world."

Nicole Ni

The Dubber & Art Designer

Ni not only dubs one character in the visual novel The Taboo Book, but also
engages in Art design including the GUI drawing and picture editing.

"I'm glad to join the ATRC Studio. I'm also glad they chose me. In my work, I will
actively keep up with my teammates and make an ideal taboo book. Although I have
never designed a game, I believe that self-confidence is the first secret of success."
